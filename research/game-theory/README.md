# Game Theory

Strategic interaction where outcomes depend on others' choices.

**In scope:** normal- and extensive-form games, Nash/correlated/Bayesian equilibria,
repeated games, mechanism design, auctions, and adversarial / multi-agent settings.

**Applications to watch:** market microstructure (order placement as a game), DeFi
mechanism design and MEV ([`../defi/`](../defi/)), and multi-agent AI
([`../ai/`](../ai/)).

> When modeling a game, state players, actions, payoffs, and information up front.
