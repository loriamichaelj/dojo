# Strategy Development

Where the other topics converge into tradable strategies.

**The pipeline:** idea → hypothesis → signal research → backtest → risk management →
(paper) execution. This folder tracks strategies end to end, including the things that kill
real strategies: transaction costs, capacity, regime shifts, and overfitting.

**Draws on:** [`../quantitative-analysis/`](../quantitative-analysis/) (signals & validation),
[`../macro-economics/`](../macro-economics/) (regimes), [`../ai/`](../ai/) (modeling),
[`../game-theory/`](../game-theory/) and [`../defi/`](../defi/) (execution & on-chain edges).

> Each strategy note should state the hypothesis, the edge's economic rationale, and how
> it was stress-tested — not just the backtest curve.
