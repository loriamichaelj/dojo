# Poker

Imperfect-information games as a laboratory for decision-making under uncertainty.

**In scope:** hand equity and pot odds, expected value, range vs. range analysis,
game-theory-optimal (GTO) play and Nash equilibria, exploitative adjustments, bankroll
management, and solver-based study.

**Applications to watch:** poker is a concrete testbed for ideas in
[`../game-theory/`](../game-theory/) (mixed strategies, bluffing as indifference,
minimax), [`../quantitative-analysis/`](../quantitative-analysis/) (variance, risk of
ruin, sample size), and [`../ai/`](../ai/) (CFR, self-play, multi-agent learning).

> When analyzing a spot, state the **stakes, positions, stacks, ranges, and board** up
> front — the poker analogue of "players, actions, payoffs, information."
